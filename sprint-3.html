Process Overview · 3D

End-to-end flow — 4 stages, 1,389 objects From left: blade decommissioning yard · twin-shaft shredder · rotary kiln with discharge flame · cyclone separator · clinker silo. Single Blender scene, organised by stage prefix, ready to slice into per-stage Unity scenes.

Per-Stage Detail

Stage 01
On-Site Treatment
Hydraulic shears cut blades to 1–2 m segments. Excavator, scattered debris, pallet base. 59 custom-built objects.
Stage 02
Mechanical Shredding
Grinder reduces to 1–20 mm particles. Twin-shaft shredder, hopper, conveyor with GFRP granules. 114 custom-built objects.
Stage 03
Thermal Decomposition Reactor
Operates at 550–660 °C. Epoxy resin decomposes to gas + char; glass fibers stay solid. 24 custom-built objects.
Stage 04
Integrated Material Separation
One cyclone-classifier tower separates gas (top), then glass fiber and char by density. Three recovered streams.
Downstream
Usage & Replacement
Glass fiber → cement clinker. Char → coal substitute. Gas → process fuel. Diverts from landfill, replaces virgin inputs.
